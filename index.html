Since 1885

Born in
Waco, Texas.

Pharmacist Charles Alderton created Dr Pepper at Morrison's Old Corner Drug Store, experimenting with fruit syrups until he landed a combination unlike anything the world had tasted.

The result was a bold, impossibly complex blend of 23 flavors — a formula so unique it defies category. Not a cola, not a root beer, not a fruit soda. Dr Pepper is simply Dr Pepper.
